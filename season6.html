Legitimate business opportunities are pursued for SAMCRO as "Sons of Anarchy" returns for its sixth series. Elsewhere, Jax (Charlie Hunnam) secures a new alliance for the club, while Clay (Ron Perlman) seeks protection. Later, the clubhouse is attacked, the guys are forced to face their past, and Jax uncovers life-altering secrets. Jimmy Smits, Kim Coates, Katey Sagal and Maggie Siff also return in Series 6.
